Overview

In today’s modernized society, the advent of the internet and computers bring about an abundance of new and easier ways of doing things, such as buying items and products online using one’s own credit card. With a simply click of a few buttons and some keystrokes, anyone could buy basically anything in the comfort of their homes. In this brave new world of convenience and accessibility, internet fraud is also on the rise.

Conclusion
