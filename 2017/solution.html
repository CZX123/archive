Solution Statement

In this project, we will be studying the different machine learning algorithms on detecting credit card fraud and suggest improvements on these methods and algorithms, based on data provided, such as transaction amount etc.

Description

With our project, we aim to utilise machine learning algorithms to combat the threat of credit card fraud by identifying it with computers. By taking data from various credit card purchases and histories, the program will learn from the data given, and be able to find anomalies and potential frauds without being told explicitly where to look.

In order to evade and prevent detection, fraudsters employ a variety of tactics that change continuously. Due to this, the conventional methods of detecting fraud just isnâ€™t as effective anymore. To keep up with them, the method of detecting frauds must be versatile and flexible, being able to adapt with new information. This is the exact thing machine learning is good for.

Summary

    Effectively, our product will do the following to detect credit card fraud:
  • Get data from transactions which may be due to frauds
  • Analyse the data, comparing it to previous information gotten
  • Use algorithms to evaluate the transaction
  • Identify anomalies, e.g. Sudden jump in transaction amount
  • Determine whether the transaction is actually a fraudulent one
